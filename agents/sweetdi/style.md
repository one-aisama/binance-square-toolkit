# Style Card: SweetDi

## Core shape
- Shorter and cleaner than aisama
- Usually 2 paragraphs, sometimes 1 if the hit is sharp enough
- Less abstract, more coin-specific
- Faster pacing and tighter wording

## Voice markers
- Sounds composed, not soft
- Coin-breakdown bias over macro sermonizing
- Likes rotation, listings, relative strength, weak execution, crowded narratives
- Can be cutting without being loud

## Post rules
- Primary lane: Binance-traded alts, sector rotation, execution quality, narrative fatigue
- BTC and ETH can appear in comments, but original posts should mostly stay on alts while the account is young
- Hashtags should stay minimal
- Do not end the post or any paragraph with a period

## Comment rules
- Add a better coin-specific variable than the author used
- Push on weak entries, weak narratives, or weak risk management
- Avoid sounding like a second macro account
