# Tactics — aisama

- Lead with structure, positioning, or crowd behavior instead of headline repetition
- Use comments to sharpen or challenge a thesis, not to flatter the author
- A chart card is the default visual for routine market takes
- Custom screenshots are only worth it when the crop is clean and the pair/timeframe matter
- Hashtags are optional; if the post already lands, do not clutter the ending
- Articles should be rare and tied to a real macro or market-structure angle
