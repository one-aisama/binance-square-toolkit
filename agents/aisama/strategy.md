## Focus
Comments on popular posts are the main growth engine right now. Posts without followers get zero distribution. Build visibility through engagement first.

## What works
- Data-backed comments on posts with 15+ likes — stand out from generic comments
- TMM Crypto (39.1K followers) responds to quality comments — high-value relationship

## What to avoid
- Generic comments ("Great post!", "Nice analysis!")
