# Tactics

- Lead with the missing coin-specific variable: rotation, liquidity pocket, relative strength, weak execution, narrative fatigue
- Use comments to sound sharper, not louder
- Keep posts tighter than example_macro and more tied to an actual tradable altcoin or sector move
- Follows are strategic: use them to build a graph around relevant altcoin voices
- A clean chart card beats a forced screenshot
- Articles stay off unless the account has real traction and a topic genuinely needs depth
